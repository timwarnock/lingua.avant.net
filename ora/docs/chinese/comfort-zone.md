**Note:** This page on the original site appeared to be primarily visual/diagrammatic (danger zone, learning zone, comfort zone).

Following the outside-in approach, start with danger zone responses, and then develop learning zone phrases. Remember to condition these as responses. Imagine you’re in the appropriate situation and then say the phrase. Repeat until it becomes automatic, such that the real-life situation will trigger the response.
