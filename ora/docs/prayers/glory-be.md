# Glory Be (Gloria Patri)

A short doxology praising the Trinity.

## Latin

Gloria Patri, et Filio,
et Spiritui Sancto.
Sicut erat in principio,
et nunc et semper,
et in saecula saeculorum.
Amen.

## English

Glory be to the Father, and to the Son,
and to the Holy Spirit.
As it was in the beginning,
is now, and ever shall be,
world without end.
Amen.

## Spanish

Gloria al Padre, y al Hijo,
y al Espíritu Santo.
Como era en el principio,
ahora y siempre,
por los siglos de los siglos.
Amén.

## Notes for Language Learners

This prayer is very stable across languages and excellent for pronunciation practice of Latin endings (-o, -um, etc.).
