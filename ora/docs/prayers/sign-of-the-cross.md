# Sign of the Cross

The Sign of the Cross is made at the beginning and end of the Rosary and most prayers.

## Latin (Traditional)

In nomine Patris, et Filii, et Spiritus Sancti. Amen.

## English

In the name of the Father, and of the Son, and of the Holy Spirit. Amen.

## Spanish

En el nombre del Padre, y del Hijo, y del Espíritu Santo. Amén.

## Practice Tip

Trace a cross on your body while reciting. This physical act helps with memorization across languages.
