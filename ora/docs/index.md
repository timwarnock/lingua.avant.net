# Ora

Praying the Rosary and traditional Christian prayers as a path to language learning.

This project revives the ancient practice of the Rosary for learners of Latin and other languages. The prayers are presented primarily in Latin with translations to aid memorization and understanding.

## The Rosary

The Rosary is a scriptural prayer that meditates on the life of Christ through the eyes of Mary.

[How to pray the Rosary](rosary.md)

## Prayers

Core prayers used in the Rosary:

- [Sign of the Cross](prayers/sign-of-the-cross.md)
- [Our Father](prayers/our-father.md)
- [Hail Mary](prayers/hail-mary.md)
- [Glory Be](prayers/glory-be.md)

Additional prayers and full mysteries will be added as the site grows.

## Languages

Content focuses on Latin (the traditional language of the Church) with English and Spanish translations to begin with. More languages coming.
