# How to Pray the Rosary

The Rosary is prayed using a string of beads while meditating on mysteries from the life of Jesus and Mary.

## Basic Steps

1. Make the Sign of the Cross.
2. Pray the Apostles' Creed.
3. Pray one Our Father.
4. Pray three Hail Marys.
5. Pray the Glory Be and Fatima Prayer.
6. Announce the first mystery and pray the decade (Our Father + 10 Hail Marys + Glory Be).
7. Repeat for 5 decades (one set of mysteries).
8. Conclude with the Hail Holy Queen and Sign of the Cross.

See the individual [prayers](prayers/index.md) for the texts.

## The Mysteries

The Rosary is divided into sets of mysteries:

- Joyful Mysteries
- Sorrowful Mysteries
- Glorious Mysteries
- Luminous Mysteries (added by Pope John Paul II)
